# Task 1 - Sales Data Analysis

## Objective
To analyze the sales dataset and generate insights on product category performance, regional sales distribution, and overall business trends.

## Dataset
- File: sales_data_sample.csv
- Source: Provided with internship task
- Contains details such as order number, product line, country, quantity ordered, price, and total sales.

## Steps Performed
1. Loaded the dataset using Pandas.
2. Performed basic cleaning and validation for numeric fields.
3. Calculated total and average sales metrics.
4. Analyzed sales by product category.
5. Analyzed sales by region (country-wise).
6. Identified top and bottom-performing categories and regions.
7. Visualized key findings using bar charts and summary tables.

## Insights from Sales Data Analysis

### 1. Overall Sales Summary
- The dataset shows strong sales performance across product lines and countries.
- Total and average sales indicate consistent customer demand.
- A noticeable gap between maximum and minimum sales reflects variations in order size and customer purchasing power.

### 2. Category-Wise Insights
- Classic Cars is the top-performing category, contributing the highest revenue.
- Classic Cars dominate overall market demand compared to other categories.
- Planes is the lowest-performing category, generating the least revenue.
- Lower-performing product lines present opportunities for targeted improvement.

### 3. Region-Wise Insights
- The USA is the best-performing region, generating the highest total sales.
- The UK is the lowest-performing region among the top contributors.
- Regions with lower performance may need marketing or product availability improvements.

### 4. Top Product Performance
- Classic Cars and Vintage Cars are the strongest revenue-driving product lines.
- These categories should be prioritized for inventory planning, stock availability, and promotional strategies.
- Lower-performing categories such as Planes may require evaluation of pricing, customer interest, or supply chain factors.

### 5. Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory and promotional efforts for Classic Cars to maximize revenue.
- Explore marketing and visibility opportunities in low-performing regions like the UK.
- Investigate potential reasons for poor sales in the Planes category.
- Strengthen presence in the USA by continuing the current sales strategies.
